Recommended range: $2,800 – $2,900  ·  Start at $2,900  ·  Adjust quickly if needed
Based on current market data for comparable 3BR/2.5BA homes in Storey Park, we recommend listing at $2,900 per month — aligned with the median leased comp and supported by your 2016 build year and 2,199 sq ft floor plan. This is a competitive, defensible price that will attract qualified applicants quickly — provided the property is show-ready at the time of listing. Condition is everything in this price range. A well-presented home at $2,900 will outperform a poorly presented home at $2,700 every single time.
Before Anything Else — The Rent-Ready Requirement
⚠ The property must be fully vacant and rent-ready before we list — no exceptions
This is the single most important factor in how quickly your property leases, what rent you achieve, and what quality of tenant you attract. We do not list occupied properties, furnished properties, or properties that are not fully move-in ready. Here is why this matters so much:

Your target tenant — the well-qualified professional or family who will pay $2,900 per month, respect your property, and renew their lease — has standards. When they walk through a home and it feels lived-in, see touch-up paint needed, or picture it full of someone else's furniture, they move on to the next property. The applicants who don't care about those things are not the applicants you want.

A show-ready home at $2,900 will lease in 14–21 days. A home that isn't ready will sit — and every day it sits is income you cannot recover.
1
Property must be fully vacantAll furniture, personal items, wall hangings, and décor must be completely removed before professional photography or showings. Tenants need to visualize their life in your home — not yours.
2
Walls must be paint-readyAt minimum, you must have the current paint color available for touch-ups. Ideally, a fresh coat of neutral paint throughout ensures photographs beautifully and signals a well-maintained property to applicants. This alone can be the difference between 14 days and 45 days on market.
3
Professional deep clean requiredThe property must be professionally cleaned to the standard we expect it returned at move-out. We photograph and document this standard so it is legally defensible at the end of the tenancy.
4
All repairs and maintenance addressedAny visible damage, non-functioning fixtures, loose hardware, or cosmetic issues must be resolved. A $50 repair ignored costs you a qualified tenant worth thousands.
5
Professional photography onlyWe do not list with phone photos. Professional photography is scheduled only when the property is fully vacant and rent-ready. Zillow ranking and online presentation are critical — your first showing happens online before anyone sets foot through the door.

The Real Cost of Vacancy
Overpricing + poor presentation = the most expensive combination in property management
One comparable in this analysis — 9759 Petition Way — sat on the market for 131 days before leasing at $3,000. That is not a market problem. That is a pricing and presentation problem. At $3,000/month, the owner lost over $13,000 in potential rental income waiting for a price that the market was not willing to pay at the speed they needed. Meanwhile, 9735 Petition Way leased in 17 days at $2,700. The difference was not the price — it was the strategy.
